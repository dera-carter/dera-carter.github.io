Fairmont Theater

Fairmont theater is a place for kids to have the opportunity to act, work, and participate in plays. In Fairmont's large opportunity, students have the chance to work in a professional, and active theater with live performances with full access to moving set pieces, flywall, sound and microphones. Unique roles are available for willing students that show interest and talent in those roles.

Past Performances

I participate in afterschool extracurriculars, which includes Technical Theater after school in preparation for performances. Our past performances include…

Harvey was the Fall play with performances Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday from November 2nd to the 4th. Harvey is a 1944 play by the American playwright Mary Chase. The play covered three acts following the infamous Elwood P. Dowd that is seeing his friend, a giant, invisible white rabbit named Harvey.

The Taming of The Shoe was the children's play performing on Saturday, April 29th of 2022 with show times at 10am and 2pm. The play written by Desiré M. Sobiech follows a band of familiar fairytale characters and the story of Mary Jane, who creates the best shoes in the land. Yet, she wears rags upon her own feet. In order to fix her dilemma, she travels with other characters to the most powerful man in the land to seek help.

A Midsummer Night's Dream was the winter play performing Thursday, Friday and Saturday from February 2nd to the 5th in 2022. Written by William Shakespeare, it follows the greek figures Theseus and Hippolyta getting married, and off somewhere else a king and queen of fairyland are trying to sort out a love triangle and more mischievous behavior.
